🗺️ Step-by-Step Admission Process
1

Qualify Entrance Examination

Register and appear for the relevant state‑level exam: EAMCET for Engineering/Pharmacy, ICET for MBA/MCA, GATE for M.Tech.

2

Online Web Counselling Registration

Visit the official state counselling portal, register with your hall ticket, and pay the counselling fee.

3

Certificate Verification

Upload scanned copies of your 10th, 12th, entrance score cards, caste certificate (if applicable), and Aadhaar.

4

Options Entry & Seat Allotment

Fill in your college and branch preferences. Seats are allotted strictly on rank, reservations, and merit.

5

Reporting & Fee Payment

Report to the university with your allotment order and original documents. Pay the semester fee at the accounts office.

6

Enrollment & ID Card

Collect your student ID, timetable, and library card. Complete your hostel registration if required.
